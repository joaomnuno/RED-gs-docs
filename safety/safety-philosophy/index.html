    Safety Philosophy¶

    State the principles that govern all safety decisions.

    • Default-to-safe: how the system behaves when uncertain or degraded.
    • Human-in-the-loop: when human confirmation is required and why.
    • Independence: separation of command, telemetry, and safety circuits.
    • Evidence: how safety claims are verified (tests, inspections, analysis).
